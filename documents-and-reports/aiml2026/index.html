An AI-native Research Ecosystem for Experimental Particle Physics

We are sharing here a community whitepaper, Building an AI‑Native Research Ecosystem for Experimental Particle Physics, and invite your participation in shaping and endorsing this vision. This document articulates a coordinated, national vision for how artificial intelligence can transform experimental particle physics across the full experimental lifecycle, from design and instrumentation to operations, analysis, and discovery. It is informed by recent community efforts and aligned with current priorities in particle physics and national investments in AI and advanced computing. We welcome comments and clarifications, particularly where the document may miss important perspectives and/or opportunities. Please send an email to us at ai-native-hep@googlegroups.com.
